Minimal Notion Icons

A set of simple icons designed to customise notes in Notion but suitable for whatever use you find.

I am not the owner nor the creator of this set of icons, I simply decided to make them available online so I can easily use them in Notion. Those familiar with Notion will understand that copying the link to the image is far easier than uploading the image over and over again.
